About

Building interfaces that teams can scale

I’m a dedicated senior/staff-level engineer with 14+ years across front-end and full-stack work, with a strong lean toward DevOps automation, architecture, and raising the bar on quality. At ESO I architect the newest NERIS Fire Incidents frontend, collaborate across data, DBA, and EMS teams, and lead legacy overhauls with React, Redux, and Radix while keeping Angular estates current.

I’ve migrated flagship products from gulp + AngularJS to webpack + Angular 15 with Confluence documentation that let six product teams upgrade faster. I mentor juniors through seniors on AI tooling (Copilot, Cursor, Claude enterprise), and I care about Figma standards, component libraries, Sonar/Snyk gating, and tests that match how teams ship.

Outside of work: travel, games, and cars — reminders that software should work for real people in messy environments.
